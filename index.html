About Me

Hi! I’m a scientist whose research interests intersect computer vision and remote sensing; using deep learning and satellite imagery to better understand our world. In mid-2022 I joined Kitware as a Senior Research Scientist on their Vision Team. Prior to this, I earned my PhD from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky in 2022, and my BS in Computer Science and Mathematics in 2016. While I was at Kentucky, I worked in the Multi-modal Vision Research Lab and was advised by Dr. Nathan Jacobs. Over the years I have interned at a number of wonderful places, including Kitware, Inc., at Oak Ridge National Laboratory, and at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Currently, I live in Richmond, VA with my wife Abbie, our dog Hopper, and our cat Jellybean.
