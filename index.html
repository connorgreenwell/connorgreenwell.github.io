Connor Greenwell

I am a second year PhD student and Research Assistant in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky. My advisor is Dr. Nathan Jacobs. My research invloves computer vision and machine learning; specifically the tasks of camera geocalibration and mapping relationships in large sets of facial imagery.

Previously, I worked as an undergraduate researcher at UKs Vis Center from January 2014 to May 2016. In the summer of 2014, I worked at UNCCs ViaLab, under the direction of Dr. Richard Souvenir, as part of a NSF REU.
