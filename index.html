About

Connor Greenwell is a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky, where he is advised by Dr. Nathan Jacobs. His research interests intersect computer vision and remote sensing; specifically using deep learning and satellite imagery to make sense of geo-temporal patterns in large scale collections of photographs. Connor earned his BS in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Kentucky in 2016. Previously he has interned at Oak Ridge National Laboratory and at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Connor lives in Lexington, KY with his wife Abbie, their two dogs Tesla and Hopper, and their cat Jellybean.
