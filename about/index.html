About Me

I am a first year PhD student and Research Assistant in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky. My advisor is Dr. Nathan Jacobs. My research invloves applying deep learning to the field of computer vision; specifically the tasks of camera geocalibration and mapping relationships in large sets of facial imagery.

Previously, I worked as an undergraduate researcher at UKs Vis Center from January 2014 to May 2016. In the summer of 2014, I worked at UNCCs ViaLab, under the direction of Dr. Richard Souvenir.

You can keep up with my work on Semantic Scholar.

Research Interests

  • Generative Models
